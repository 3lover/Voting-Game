Welcome to the Voting Game! This is a quick tutorial of how to play.
At the start of a round, a card is either drawn from the host's pile, or written by a player. For example, lets say the card
"Who is most likely to go to jail?"
is drawn.
Each player votes the person they think best fits the card by clicking on their name. When everyone is done voting, you move on to the guessing phase.
During the guessing phase, each player gets the number of players who voted them, but not who. Players make a guess for every vote they got as to who voted them.
For example, say you got 2 votes. During the guessing phase, you would guess two other players as your voters.
After everyone with votes guesses, player votes are revealed and players are given points. The point method differs by lobby, but in general the more correct guessed you got the better.
After points are assigned the round ends, and new rounds are played until everyone gets bored and leaves.
